Projects

Software

  • Galatea : A cross-platform tool to control MIDI synthesizers using a graphic tablet or a mouse.
  • Voks : An instrument for vocal synthesis controlled with gestures. Designed with Xiao Xiao during my PhD, supervised by Christophe d'Alessandro and Boris Doval.

Voks won the second prize of the prestigious Guthman Musical Instrument Competition, whose final took place on the Georgia Tech campus in March 2022.

  • Amati : A VST plugin for audio effect live-coding in Faust.
