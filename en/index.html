About

Welcome to my webpage! I'm an engineer and musician, passionate about music, mathematics and computer science. I'm especially interested in the building and use of digital tools for artistic creation.

I defended my Ph.D. thesis, then worked as a research engineer, at Sorbonne Université, at LAM. Before that, I graduated from the Centrale Paris general engineering school. I'm currently working as a research engineer at LAM, where I carry on my work on vocal synthesis. Besides, I play the violin and the piano, I sing and make music on my computer.

You can see my projects on Github, and listen to a few of my compositions on my Youtube channel or my Soundcloud profile. If you happen to wonder about the music that I listen to myself, feel free to check out my long Spotify playlist.

I'm looking for a job right now! I would like to work as an engineer on digital projects pertaining to artistic creation in the broad sense (not necessarily music!). Feel free to send me an email at gregoireloc (at) gmail.com (in French, English, or German). You can also find me on Linkedin.
